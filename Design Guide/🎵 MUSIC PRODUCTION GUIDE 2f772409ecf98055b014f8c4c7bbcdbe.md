## **Your Music Pipeline:**

### **DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) Choice:**

**Free options:**

- **GarageBand** (Mac) - Easiest start
- **Reaper** (Trial forever) - Professional
- **LMMS** - Open source

**Paid ($200):**

- **FL Studio** - Industry standard, great for learning

---

### **Track Creation (Per Track: 6-10 hours):**

**Hour 1-2: Melody**

- Hum/play on piano
- Record MIDI notes
- Basic chord progression

**Hour 3-4: Arrangement**

- Add drums
- Bass line
- Counter-melodies

**Hour 5-7: Instrumentation**

- Choose virtual instruments
- Layer sounds
- Texture building

**Hour 8-9: Mixing**

- EQ (balance frequencies)
- Compression (even volumes)
- Reverb/effects

**Hour 10: Export & Test**

- Export WAV/OGG
- Test in game
- Adjust volume

---

### **Total Music Time:**

**Tracks needed:**

- Main theme: 10 hours
- 3 region themes: 20 hours
- Boss theme: 10 hours
- Shop/menu themes: 10 hours
- Victory/defeat: 5 hours
- **Total: ~55 hours music**

---

### **Sound Effects (Faster):**

**Option 1:** Use **freesound.org** (free, high quality)

- 90% of indie games do this
- Legal, royalty-free

**Option 2:** Create with tools:

- **BFXR** (free, 8-bit sounds)
- **ChipTone** (free, retro sounds)
- Record real sounds (sword = hit pots together!)

**Time: 10-15 hours total for SFX**
